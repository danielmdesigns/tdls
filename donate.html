Simplist is built by a small, one man team, dedicated to bring you frequent free feature updates. We’d Love to know how we can make you even more productive, so get in touch via the Website “Support” link, or tweet us @SimpList

My goal was to create a todo app that is beautiful and easy to use. An app that really helps you save time, achieve goals, and complete tasks.

By downloading this app you support my development and my education. Every person who downloads this app makes me happy and enables me to continue working on this app.
